Golden Retriever Planet

Golden Retrievers are known for their sweet disposition and desire to be near their owners. They are naturally bred to retrieve whatever their owners desire and love to please. Beautiful dogs known for their iconic golden coat; one is hard-pressed to think of a better companion at your side every day.

Golden Qualities

  • Appearance

    The Golden Retriever is a sturdy, muscular dog of medium size; famous for the dense, lustrous coat of gold that gives the breed its name.
    The broad head, with its friendly and intelligent eyes, short ears, and straight muzzle, is a breed hallmark.

  • Demeanor

    The golden retriever is even-tempered, intelligent and affectionate.
    Golden retrievers are playful, yet gentle with children, and they tend to get along well with other pets and strangers.
